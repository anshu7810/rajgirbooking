Zoo Safari Rajgir Rules and Regulations

  • Matches and Light or flammable products are prohibited in the zoo.
  • Any kind of intoxicant, cigarette, alcohol, gutkha, paan, and others will not be allowed in the zoo.
  • Gadgets of any kind that generate loud noises are not permitted.
  • Sharp objects such as cutters or knives are not permitted.
  • No one is permitted to throw garbage in the zoo.
  • No one is permitted to go near the animals.
  • Food can be brought in but is only permitted in the cafeteria area.
  • All items such as ladies’ purses have to be deposited in the office.
